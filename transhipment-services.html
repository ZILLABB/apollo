Transhipment Services

APOLLO LOGISTICS BV possesses efficient facilities for transhipment logistics. We combine multiple modes of transportation, such as long-distance or local vehicles, seagoing ships, inland waterway vessels, or rail transport, to deliver your goods and items swiftly and dependably. Transhipment logistics is a key component of supply chains and is therefore included in the range of products offered by APOLLO LOGISTICS BV, a full-service provider, in addition to transport and warehousing services. Our staff members make sure that cars are loaded and unloaded promptly. For this reason, APOLLO LOGISTICS BV own hubs, branches, and terminals are outfitted with effective transhipment equipment. In order to handle unique tasks in the field of project logistics, we collaborate with capable partners; for example, they might have specialized cranes for loading and unloading large objects.
